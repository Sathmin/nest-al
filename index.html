Privacy Policy

At A\L Nest, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information:

Information Collection

We only collect necessary information to provide our services, such as basic account details and usage statistics.

Data Usage

Your information is used solely to enhance your learning experience and improve our platform.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Third Parties

Our Vision

A\L Nest was founded with a clear vision: to democratize access to quality education for all Sri Lankan students preparing for their Advanced Level examinations.

We believe that every student deserves equal opportunities to excel, regardless of their location or economic background. Our platform brings together the best educational resources, expert guidance, and innovative learning tools in one accessible place.

Our mission is to transform the traditional learning experience by leveraging technology to create an engaging, personalized, and effective educational environment that helps students achieve their academic dreams.

Quality Education

Providing high-quality, accessible education to all students across Sri Lanka.

Innovation

Leveraging technology to create innovative learning solutions.

Accessibility

Ensuring all students have equal access to educational resources.
